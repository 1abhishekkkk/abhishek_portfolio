## 🛠️ Technical Implementation

### **Step 1: Get Your Avatar File**
Choose one of the methods above and download your avatar as:
- **Format**: GLB (preferred) or GLTF
- **Size**: Under 5MB for web performance
- **Name**: `avatar.glb` (recommended)

### **Step 2: Add to Your Website**
```bash
# Place your avatar file
cp your-avatar.glb public/models/avatar.glb
```

### **Step 3: Update the Code**
I'll help you update the Avatar3D.jsx to use your model:

```javascript
// In Avatar3D.jsx, update the default modelPath
<HeroAvatar modelPath="/models/avatar.glb" />
<FloatingAvatar modelPath="/models/avatar.glb" />
```
