**Widget services solve this** by handling everything for you:
- ✅ No coding needed
- ✅ Auto-updates
- ✅ No API management
- ✅ Free plans available
- ✅ 5-minute setup

## 📝 Step-by-Step: Elfsight (Easiest)

### **1. Create Widget:**

```
1. Visit: https://elfsight.com/instagram-feed-instashow/
2. Click "Add to Website" or "Try for Free"
3. Sign up with email
4. Click "Create New Widget"
5. Choose "Instagram Feed"
```

### **2. Connect Instagram:**

```
1. Click "Connect Instagram Account"
2. Login with your Instagram (@abhi_clicks._)
3. Allow permissions
4. Select your account
```

### **3. Customize:**

```
Feed Settings:
- Layout: Grid (recommended)
- Columns: 3 (desktop), 2 (mobile)
- Posts to show: 6-9
- Spacing: Medium

Design:
- Theme: Dark (to match your portfolio)
- Border radius: 12px
- Hover effect: Zoom

Colors:
- Background: Transparent or #000000
- Text: #FFFFFF
- Links: #a855f7 (purple)
```

### **4. Get Embed Code:**

```
1. Click "Publish" or "Get Code"
2. Copy the embed code
3. It looks like:

<script src="https://apps.elfsight.com/p/platform.js" defer></script>
<div class="elfsight-app-xxxxx-xxxxx"></div>
```

### **5. Send Me the Code:**

Once you have the code, send it to me and I'll integrate it into your portfolio instantly!
